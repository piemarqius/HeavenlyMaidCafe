Midnight Maids

Welcome to The Midnight Maid Cafe, your gateway to the heart of NYC's nocturnal Moe magic.

Dive into a world where the night comes alive, where every moment is an exploration of magical delights. As the 18/21+ sister brand to the beloved Heavenly Maid Cafe, we bring a unique twist to the traditional Japanese-inspired Maid Cafe experience. Our enchanting Maids and Butlers are here to host unforgettable events, perform mesmerizing shows, and make special appearances that will leave you spellbound.

Whether you're looking to host a themed party, need top-notch bartending services, or simply want to immerse yourself in a whimsical night out, The Midnight Maid Cafe is your ultimate destination. Join us and let the Moe magic of the night captivate your senses.
