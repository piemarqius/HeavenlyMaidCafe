Heavenly Maid Cafe

At the Heavenly Maid Cafe, we are proud to bring you the most kawaii experience of your life.

  • Experience enchanting maid performances,
  • Taste the most delicious treats,
  • Have the most delightful service,
  • And our special Moe magic.

Step into our world of joy and let us create unforgettable moments for you!

Heavenly Maid Cafe is an immersive and memorable dining experience that celebrates Japanese hospitality and Kawaii culture with a unique NYC flair. Our mission is simple yet impactful: to uplift those around us and spread positivity across New York City!

We welcome our guests as royalty, and treat them as such. Our maid cafes include: delicious treats & sweets, amazing live performances from our talented Maids & butlers, table side service with adorable chocolate/ketchup art drawn for guests, interactive & delightful games, adorable photo opportunities and much more!

Our Japanese-inspired maid cafe is a delightful blend of tradition and entertainment, offering a unique and charming experience like no other. Our group stands out as feature guests and performers because we are masters of creating an atmosphere of warmth, friendliness, and enchantment. With our team's impeccable attention to detail, captivating cosplay, and engaging interactions, we transport our guests to a world of fantasy and hospitality. Whether it's through our impeccable tea service, adorable performances, or the authentic ambiance we create, we ensure that every visitor feels like they've stepped into a charming slice of Japan. As feature guests, we promise to bring an unforgettable touch of magic to any event or venue, leaving guests with cherished memories that will last a lifetime.
